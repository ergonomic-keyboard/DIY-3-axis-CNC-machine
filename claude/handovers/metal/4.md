## Key gotchas I hit (save you re-discovering)

1. **`sketch.setExpression` does NOT update the constraint's solved value.** You must also call `sketch.setDatum(ci, FreeCAD.Units.Quantity(f'{value} mm'))` to seed it. Otherwise the geometry stays where the placeholder put it and the expression silently does nothing until the next manual GUI edit.

2. **Spreadsheet row scan**: don't `break` on the first empty cell, scan up to row ~100 and just `pass` on exceptions. v6→v7 first attempt hit a blank gap row and overwrote `motor_x`/`motor_y` with new params. Result: H20 jumped to (15.5, 1.75).

3. **Sketcher axis & root-point GeoIds**: RootPoint = (-1, 1), H-axis line = (-1, 0), V-axis line = (-2, 0). For Symmetric-about-a-line use `Sketcher.Constraint('Symmetric', g1, p1, g2, p2, -2)` (only 5 args; the line has no PosId).

4. **Constraint type IDs** (from FreeCAD source): 1=Coincident, 2=Horizontal, 3=Vertical, 6=Distance, 7=DistanceX, 8=DistanceY, 11=Radius, 12=Equal, 14=Symmetric.

5. **PointPos for circles**: the centre is `PosId = 3` (mid). Always select the centre, never the curve, when feeding distance/symmetric constraints.

6. **Run scripts with** `freecadcmd /tmp/build_vN.py`. Tail the last 25–30 lines — earlier "Importing project files……" progress noise is harmless.

7. **CARRIAGE_drawing.txt** at `docs/stl_drawings/router/CARRIAGE_drawing.txt` is the source of truth for the H## labels. Plate centre for STL→sketch translation: `(428.89, 95.0)` (mean of `[plate_x_min, plate_x_max]` and `[plate_z_min, plate_z_max]` from `2_flattened_image/holes_from_stl.json`).
