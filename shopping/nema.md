### If this is for a CNC mill or router

I would choose:

* **Direct-drive axes (X/Y):** STEPPERONLINE **17HS19-2004S1**
* **Rotary axis or high-torque reduction:** Joy-IT **NEMA17-07GM** (EAN **4250236821740**)
